About

Reasons to choose

Hi! I’m Merlin Quadros. I love making art. My passion started from a young age when I would help my Cousin paint the walls in his house. Then as I grew older, my talent also grew and I learnt how to do many things. That is when I started Galeria De Decoracão.

I hand craft each item with love and care to curate beautiful, unique and elegant decorations that will guarantee to sparkle on your special occasion. So if you're looking for show-stopping decoration, let's talk! Tell me what you are looking for and I'll tell you how I can create the decoration you've been dreaming of.
